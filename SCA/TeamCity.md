Set up TeamCity to scan your manifests with SOOS SCA Core.

## Prerequisites
- You need to have a [SOOS account](https://app.soos.io/register).
- Node 22 LTS or higher enabled on the pipeline.

## Steps

### **Get the Example**

* Navigate to the [TeamCity SCA integration page on the SOOS App](https://app.soos.io/integrate/sca?id=teamcity), copy the example, and modify it.

### **Run It**

* Execute the pipeline
